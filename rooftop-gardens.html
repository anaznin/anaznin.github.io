Rooftop Gardens

Why start a rooftop garden?

  • Rooftop gardens are great features to transform urban homes into peaceful and eco-friendly environments.
  • If you live in an apartment or a house without a backyard, rooftop gardens would give you the opportunity to tend a variety of plants ranging from trees and grasses, to fruits and vegetables.
  • Make sure that your roof is legible for cultivating plants on. i.e, sun exposure, water drainage
  • Get creative! Choose the right seeds or bulbs to utilize your space efficiently.

How can you get one started?

  • Make sure that your roof/balcony is legible for cultivating plants on. i.e, sun exposure, water drainage
  • Conduct some research when buying fertilizer,soil, pots, and seeds for gardening
  • Get creative! Gather your choice of seeds or bulbs to utilize your space efficiently.
  • Grab some gloves and start planting!
